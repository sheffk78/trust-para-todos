Article I
Declaration and Revocation of Prior Wills

Section 1.1 Declaration. I, {{settlor_1_full_name}}, of {{settlor_1_city}}, {{governing_state}}, declare this to be my Last Will and Testament.

Section 1.2 Revocation. I hereby revoke all wills and codicils previously made by me.

Article II
Identification of Testator

I am of legal age, of sound mind, and under no duress or undue influence. I reside at {{settlor_1_address}}, {{settlor_1_city}}, {{governing_state}}. I am married to {{spouse_full_name}}, and I have {{number_of_children}} children, namely: {{children_names}}.

Article III
Nomination of Executor

Section 3.1 Nomination. I nominate and appoint {{executor_name}}, of {{executor_address}}, as Executor of this Will. If {{executor_name}} is unable or unwilling to serve, I nominate {{alternate_executor_name}}, of {{alternate_executor_address}}, as Alternate Executor.

Section 3.2 Independent Administration. I request that my Executor be permitted to serve as independent representative without court supervision to the maximum extent permitted by law. No bond or other security shall be required of my Executor.

Section 3.3 Executor Acceptance. My Executor shall not be required to qualify or to give any bond or other security in any jurisdiction.

Article IV
Pour-Over Clause

Section 4.1 Residuary Bequest to Trust. I give, devise, and bequeath all of my estate, both real and personal, and wherever situated, including all property that I may own or have the right to dispose of at the time of my death, to the Trustee of the {{trust_name}}, dated {{trust_date}}, and any amendments thereto, to be held and administered in accordance with the terms of said Trust. This bequest includes all property that I may acquire after the date of this Will and all property not otherwise effectively disposed of by this Will.

Section 4.2 Incorporation by Reference. The terms of the {{trust_name}} are incorporated herein by reference. The disposition of my estate under this Will shall be governed by the terms of said Trust as they exist at the time of my death.

Section 4.3 Failure of Trust. If the Trust described above is not in existence at the time of my death, or if the Trustee fails to qualify or accept the trust, I direct that the property otherwise passing to the Trust shall be held and administered by my Executor in a trust having terms identical to those of the Trust, and my Executor shall serve as Trustee thereof.

Article V
Specific Bequests

Section 5.1 Tangible Personal Property. I may leave a written statement or memorandum, separate from this Will, disposing of certain items of tangible personal property. If such a statement exists at the time of my death, I direct my Executor to distribute such property in accordance with that statement. If no such statement exists, I give all of my tangible personal property to the Trustee of the Trust described in Article IV, to be distributed in accordance with the terms of said Trust.

Section 5.2 Specific Gifts. I make the following specific gifts:

  1. (a) {{specific_bequest_1}}
  2. (b) {{specific_bequest_2}}
  3. (c) {{specific_bequest_3}}

If a beneficiary named in this Article does not survive me by one hundred twenty (120) hours, the gift to that beneficiary shall lapse and become part of the residue of my estate.

Article VI
Nomination of Guardian for Minor Children

Section 6.1 Guardian of the Person. If I have minor children at the time of my death, and if the other parent of my children is unable or unwilling to serve as guardian, I nominate {{guardian_name}}, of {{guardian_address}}, as guardian of the person of my minor children. If {{guardian_name}} is unable or unwilling to serve, I nominate {{alternate_guardian_name}}, of {{alternate_guardian_address}}, as guardian of the person of my minor children.

Section 6.2 Guardian of the Estate. I nominate the same person(s) named above as guardian of the estate of my minor children. If a guardian of the estate is required, I request that such guardian serve without bond.

Article VII
Executor Powers

Section 7.1 General Grant of Powers. My Executor shall have all powers granted to personal representatives under the laws of the State of {{governing_state}}, and in addition, without limitation, the following powers, all of which may be exercised without court order or approval:

  1. (a) To sell, lease, exchange, or otherwise dispose of any real or personal property of my estate, at public or private sale, on such terms as my Executor deems advisable.
  2. (b) To retain any property of my estate without liability for loss or depreciation, and without the need to diversify.
  3. (c) To invest and reinvest estate funds in any property, real or personal, as my Executor deems prudent.
  4. (d) To borrow money and to mortgage, pledge, or encumber any property of my estate as security.
  5. (e) To continue any business of mine for a reasonable period, and to incorporate, dissolve, or sell any business interest.
  6. (f) To employ attorneys, accountants, investment advisors, and other professionals, and to pay their reasonable compensation from my estate.
  7. (g) To compromise, settle, or abandon any claims or causes of action in favor of or against my estate.
  8. (h) To distribute estate property in cash or in kind, or partly in cash and partly in kind, and to determine the value of any property distributed in kind.
  9. (i) To make distributions to a minor beneficiary by paying funds to the beneficiary’s parent, guardian, or custodian under the Uniform Transfers to Minors Act.
  10. (j) To execute and deliver all documents necessary to carry out the administration of my estate.
Article VIII
Tax Provisions

Section 8.1 Payment of Taxes. All estate, inheritance, and succession taxes payable by reason of my death, including any interest and penalties thereon, shall be paid from the residue of my estate passing under this Will, without apportionment among beneficiaries and without reimbursement from any beneficiary.

Section 8.2 Tax Elections. My Executor is authorized to make any and all tax elections permitted by law, including the election to treat certain property as qualified terminable interest property under Section 2056(b)(7) of the Internal Revenue Code, and to allocate any available generation-skipping transfer tax exemption.

Article IX
Simultaneous Death

Section 9.1 Survival Requirement. No beneficiary under this Will shall be entitled to receive any distribution unless that beneficiary survives me by one hundred twenty (120) hours. If a beneficiary fails to survive for such period, the beneficiary shall be deemed to have predeceased me for all purposes of this Will.

Section 9.2 Simultaneous Death with Spouse. If my spouse and I die under circumstances in which the order of death cannot be established, each of us shall be deemed to have survived the other for purposes of this Will.

Article X
No-Contest Clause

If any beneficiary under this Will or under the Trust referenced herein, or any person claiming through such beneficiary, contests the validity of this Will or the Trust, or any provision thereof, or files a petition to remove my Executor without reasonable cause, then that beneficiary’s interest under this Will shall be forfeited, and the property otherwise passing to that beneficiary shall be distributed as if that beneficiary had predeceased me.

Article XI
Governing Law

This Will shall be construed and interpreted according to the laws of the State of {{governing_state}}. If any provision of this Will is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.
